Why I Choose

Product Management

Because My moto is customer satisfaction.

After several years of experience as software developer, I decided didn't want to be doing the same thing when I will be 30 years old. As I started considering different positions, I was continually drawn to the product manager (PM) role because of following traits:

  • 1. Vision and Discipline

    I have to know what’s important to solve, who am I solving it for, why is it worth solving, and how do I measure success?

  • 2. Story Teller

    I used to be the best story teller to your team, to your customers and to the market. They should know what I’m building, and more importantly, why I’m building it.

  • 3. Win with a Team

    A collaborative mindset is key. I am responsible for defining the requirements with cross functional teams to design, implement, test, refine and deploy this new addition to the roadmap.
